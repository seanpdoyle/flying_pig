About:

Flying Pig Picture Frames is a solo operation run by Dan Tag, a true artist and picture framer for over 30 years, offering quality affordable framing with old-school service.

How it works:

To keep up with demand and a quick turnaround, Dan makes frames off premises at his wood shop. The Philadelphia Showroom is only open to the public for retail Thursday through Saturday from 1:00 PM until 6:00 PM.

What to do:

Come see why Dan was awarded "Best of Philly" from Philadelphia Magazine and be a part of his loyal following.
